Secure Your Binance Account

At Binance, we take your security seriously. We're constantly updating our security measures to ensure your assets remain safe and protected.

Why Choose a Non-Custodial Wallet?

A non-custodial wallet, such as TrustWallet, offers you full control over your assets and private keys, minimizing reliance on third-party storage. You are the sole owner of your funds.

How to Activate Your Trust Wallet

Follow these steps to set up your Trust Wallet securely:

  1. Download the Trust Wallet app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  2. Securely store your recovery phrase. This phrase is critical for wallet access and should never be shared with anyone, including Binance representatives.
  3. During setup, select [I already have a wallet] and input the recovery phrase from the box below.

Security Best Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all of your accounts.
